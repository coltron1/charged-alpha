What is this tool?

The Crypto Screener lets you filter cryptocurrencies by price, market capitalization, recent performance, and trading volume. Use the preset buttons to quickly find large-cap coins, recent gainers or losers, or high-volume tokens. Click any result row to see a price chart. This tool helps you narrow down thousands of cryptocurrencies to find ones matching your criteria.

Reading Your Results

  • Market Cap — The total value of all circulating coins. Larger market caps generally mean more stability; smaller caps are riskier but may offer higher growth potential.
  • 24h & 7d Change — Short-term momentum indicators. Green means the price is rising, red means falling. Compare both to see if a move is a brief spike or sustained trend.
  • Volume — High trading volume confirms that price movement has real participation behind it. Low volume moves are more likely to reverse.
  • Circ/Total % — Shows how much of the total coin supply is already in circulation. A low ratio means more coins could be released over time, potentially diluting value.
  • Click any row to see a price chart — use the 7D/30D/90D/1Y buttons to see how the coin has performed over different timeframes.
