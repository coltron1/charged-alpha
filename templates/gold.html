What is this tool?

This aggregator searches live listings for gold, silver, and platinum across eBay (top-rated sellers only), SD Bullion, and optionally person-to-person marketplaces like Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace. Every listing is enriched with detected weight, purity, and metal content so you can instantly see how each price compares to the current spot price â helping you find the best deals on physical precious metals.

How to use: Choose your metal (Gold, Silver, or Platinum) using the tabs above, set any filters you want (type, purity, price range, weight range), then click Search. Results are sorted by price and show the premium or discount vs. spot price for each listing. Use the "Best Deal" sort to find the lowest premiums. Check the person-to-person checkbox to include Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace results.
