What is this tool?

The Forex (foreign exchange) Heatmap shows how major currency pairs are performing against each other. Currencies are always traded in pairs â for example, EUR/USD means you're comparing the Euro to the US Dollar. Green cells mean the first currency is strengthening against the second; red means it's weakening. The Currency Strength Index at the bottom aggregates all pairs to show which currencies are strongest and weakest overall. Use the timeframe buttons to compare short-term vs. long-term trends. Click any cell to see a detailed price chart.

Darker green = stronger gain, darker red = bigger loss. Click any pair to see its price chart over time.
